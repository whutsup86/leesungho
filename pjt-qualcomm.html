Background

Qualcomm has released Aware Tracker which is a smart device with significant sensor capabilities that track the location and enviromental condition of assests, such as temperature, humidity, pressure, impact, and more.

Specification Description
Dimension(mm) 50 x 125 x 14
Weight(g) 125
Operating Range Temperature: -20℃ to 60℃ (when discharging), 5℃ to 60℃ (when charging) Relative Humidity (RH): 85% non-condensing

Wireless Communication LTE(Cat.M1),LTE(Cat.NB2),Wi-Fi,GNSS

Big Question : What values can the Qualcomm Tracker provide for Rakuten Services?

Rakuten group's 70+ business provide a broad range of online and offline services including e-commerce, travel, digital content, fintech etc. The team initiated exploring business idea with a lot of services in Rakuten group. Particularly, we're focusing on the business areas where data-driven decision and time-critical tracking are required. Finally, we found a couple of opportunity and suggested collboration as follows:

  • Collboration A
  • Rakuten Yoyakusuri X Qualcomm
    • Requirement: Safe medicine delivery with temperature monitoring
    • Visualization: Blender, Adobe Illustrator
    • Front-end: HTML/CSS/Java
    • Back-end: Qualcomm Aware API
    • Data Framework: Python
  • Collboration B
  • Rakuten Medical X Qualcomm
    • Requirement: Safe equipment delivery with shock detection monitoring
    • Visualization: Blender, Adobe Illustrator
    • Front-end: HTML/CSS/Java
    • Back-end: Qualcomm Aware API
    • Data Framework: Python
