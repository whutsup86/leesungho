Remote Retail Shop

People were hesitant to go outside during COVID-19, which affected all offline shops. Mobile retail shops, in particular, experienced a significant reduction in mobile contracts. Remote shops allow people to complete contracts without visiting physical stores. They offer end-to-end services, including product introductions, contract processing, and delivery online. Customers can choose a remote shop based on their preference and connect with operators through online conferences

2019

Hug2Go

Hug2Go is a personal mobility vehicle designed for indoor use. Its concept was inspired by the experience of sitting backwards in a chair. The vehicle resembles a chair, allowing you to sit in the seat. When you want to move, you can sit facing backwards and hug the back of the chair. You can control left and right turns by adjusting the seat back. Additionally, it features autonomous driving at low speeds until it detects the user.
